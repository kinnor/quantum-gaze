Industry-Specific EDI Solutions

Tailored integration platforms for your sector

Retail & E-Commerce

Seamless integration with retailers and marketplaces. Support for EDI 850/855/856/810, ASN automation, inventory synchronization, and omnichannel order management. Connect with Amazon, Walmart, Target, and 10,000+ retailers.

Logistics & Transportation

Real-time shipment tracking, freight audit automation, and carrier integration. Support for EDI 204/214/990, IoT sensor data, and predictive delivery analytics. Seamless integration with CargoWise, Oracle TMS, SAP TM, Blue Yonder, Manhattan WMS, and other major TMS/WMS platforms.

Healthcare & Pharma

HIPAA-compliant EDI for claims (837), remittance (835), eligibility (270/271), and chargeback processing. Support for NCPDP SCRIPT, HL7 FHIR integration, and real-time benefit verification.

Manufacturing & Distribution

Supply chain orchestration with EDI 830/862/866 for demand forecasting, JIT inventory, and supplier collaboration. Integration with PLM, MES, and ERP systems for end-to-end visibility.

Automotive & Aerospace

Tier-1 supplier integration with OEM-specific requirements. Support for AIAG standards, ASN with VIN/serial tracking, and supplier portal automation. Connect with COVISINT, ANX, and private networks.

Financial Services

Secure payment processing, bank reconciliation, and regulatory reporting. Support for ISO 20022, SWIFT, ACH, and wire transfer automation with fraud detection and AML compliance.
